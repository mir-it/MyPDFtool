Merge PDF tab (FOR TC TEAM AND APPOLO TEAM)
This tab merges several packing-slip PDFs into one large file.
Why? The India team sends PDFs with at most about 100 packing slips per file; you may get several files — this makes later steps easier. The next tabs make this obvious.

Split orders / samples tab (FOR APPOLO TEAM)
This tab splits merged files from “Merge PDF” into two documents: samples only and orders only, so you avoid manual work and save time.
PS: Pay close attention to where output files are saved.

Packing slips + labels tab (FOR APPOLO TEAM)
After the steps above you should have something like merged_output_samples. If you merge it on this tab with the daily THD Samples PDF from the India team, labels are indexed in order and aligned with order numbers.
